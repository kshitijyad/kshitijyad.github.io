About Me

Kshitij Yadav


Masters in Business Analytics, conc in Data Science and Marketing

University of Texas at Dallas

Hi, My name is Kshitij Yadav. I am a graduate student at University of Texas at Dallas, pursuing my Masters in Business Analytics with concentration in Data Science and Marketing Analytics. As a current graduate student at Naveen Jindal School of Management at the University of Texas at Dallas, I have garnered a deep and foundational academic base of knowledge but have also obtained practical professional experience in the business field. In charting my rise from an early Business Associate to my most recent role as an Data Science Intern with Colaberry, MA, United States, my experience is substantive and multifaceted. In my recent role with Colaberry, I garnered deep experience utilizing machine learning to identify interests of its customers and increase its revenue in its 3rd Quarter by 9%. I was also tasked with building a web scraper tool using Natural Language Processing and advance machine learning tools in python to screen through a candidate’s profile using its resume and develop a custom profile, which was then presented to Colaberry’s clients to its HR. Finally, I also worked as a Business Development Manager, helping Colaberry to expand the reach of its product called Refactored.ai, a data science platform, in many universities across United States and India. Thus, with an academic and practical history steeped in the marriage of analysis and business leadership, I am immersed in the trends shaping the industry.

Prior to my internship at Colaberry, I established a successful record of advancement in a field of analytics with ZS Associates. Starting as an Operation Associate, I worked to develop an Incentive Compensation system using SQL, which was used to calculate compensation for more than 3 thousand sales representatives for multi-billion pharmaceutical clients. I then progressed to my role in analytics, where I worked in the field data analytics to provide insights to my clients on its internal data, helping them in marketing their product. I was also successful in securing $12 Million funding of client renewal business for which I was awarded Operational Excellence Award which is eloquent of my business understanding. I take pride in the work I do and I really enjoy exploring data and crunching numbers to find some sense in them. Thanks to these and other experiences, I possess an essential skill set well suited to address the duties and responsibilities of the Data Scientist/Data Analyst/Marketing Analyst.
